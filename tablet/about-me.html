Hi, my name is Kevin Dale, I am a graphic designer/artworker and I have a passion for design and technology. I have extensive knowledge of the apple mac system and I am also a keen amateur photographer.

 

I first became interested in art and design from an early age and went on to obtain a BA (Hons) in graphic design at the Surrey Institute of Art and Design.

I have worked in a number of companies over the last 22 years dealing with various types of print and online graphics and believe that getting an understanding of my clients and their business is paramount in producing the best designs possible for the job.

 

I am a designer obsessed with emerging technology and love to push the boundaries in design.

For the past 18+ years I have provided Mac technical support to a team of artworkers and graphic designers as well as designing and artworking myself.

 

I have had past experience running to an image setter before sending to print as well as other reprographic duties, although these days film is no longer required and it is run straight to plate.

 

I have a vast experience in dealing with marketing material for point of sale, packaging design, 3D visualising concepts and creating digital online graphics.
